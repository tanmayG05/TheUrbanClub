Help & FAQs

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Signup" button on the homepage, fill in the required details, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, you can update your profile information by logging into your account and accessing the profile settings. Here, you can change your details and preferences.
